About Me

I am a house music producer and DJ. I use the moniker Shōdan. I am from East New York, Brooklyn, NYC. Instead of turning to the streets that where full of crime and corruption. I used music as my escape.

I fell in love in love with house music during the golden era of the Paradise Garage and the Paladium. I had a few chances to become something more that an avid fan. But, I choice corporate America. (Health care insurance makes for a complelling argument.)

Serveral years later here I am. Making and mixing the music I wanted to make decades ago.
